Match a Word:
Task: Write a regular expression to match any word (sequence of letters).
Test String: "Hello, World!"
Regular Expression: \w+

Extract Email Addresses:
Task: Extract all email addresses from a given text.
Test String: "Contact support at support@example.com and john.doe123@company.co.uk."
Regular Expression: (\w+\.*\w+.)\@(\w+\.*\w+\.*\w+)

Validate Phone numbers:
Task: Validate a phone number in the format (XXX) XXX-XXXX.
Test String: "Call us at (123) 456-7890 or (987) 654-3210.
Regular Expression: \(\d{3}\)\s\d{3}-\d{4}
